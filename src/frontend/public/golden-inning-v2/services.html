Services

Consultancy Services

End-to-end advisory for construction chemicals manufacturing

Phase 1

Project Setup Advisory

Comprehensive support for promoters establishing new construction chemicals manufacturing plants.

  • Plant planning — Site selection, plant layout design, and capacity planning aligned with business objectives
  • Machinery selection — Identifying and sourcing the right mixing, filling, and processing equipment for your product range
  • Production process setup — Establishing standard operating procedures, raw material specifications, and batch production workflows
  • Raw material sourcing — Guidance on supplier identification, quality benchmarks, and cost-effective procurement
  • Trial production support — On-site support during initial production runs to troubleshoot and optimise early output

From greenfield planning to first production batch — we guide every step.

Phase 2

Production Stabilisation

Post-commissioning support to stabilise operations and build consistent quality output.

  • Process optimisation — Reducing variability, improving cycle times, and increasing output per batch
  • Quality systems — Implementing in-process quality checks, finished goods testing protocols, and documentation
  • Production efficiency improvement — Labour deployment, equipment utilisation, and shift planning
  • Waste reduction — Identifying material loss points and implementing controls to reduce input waste
  • Troubleshooting — Addressing formulation issues, equipment bottlenecks, and process inconsistencies

Typical plants achieve stable production within 60–90 days of commissioning with structured support.

Phase 3

Business Advisory

Strategic guidance to position your brand, launch products, and build distribution networks.

  • Product launch strategy — Positioning products for your target market, pricing guidance, and launch sequencing
  • Market positioning — Identifying competitive advantages and crafting a consultancy-led brand narrative
  • Distribution development — Building dealer and contractor networks, trade channel strategy
  • Brand and packaging advisory — Product naming, technical data sheet structure, label guidance
  • Regulatory and compliance — Understanding relevant quality standards and certifications required in your market
