About Golden Inning

Golden Inning is a professional consultancy platform led by Devraj Gohil, providing advisory services for construction chemical manufacturing businesses.

With over 34 years of industry leadership experience, the consultancy supports promoters, investors and manufacturers in setting up and growing construction chemical manufacturing operations.

Core Consultancy Services

Manufacturing Plant Setup

Advisory support for establishing construction chemical manufacturing units including dry mix plants, production layout planning and machinery selection.

Product Portfolio Development

Guidance in developing product range such as tile adhesives, waterproofing products, wall putty and other construction chemicals.

Production Stabilisation

Support for improving manufacturing performance including process optimisation, quality control systems and cost efficiency.

Business Growth Strategy

Strategic advisory for product positioning, market entry planning and distribution strategy for the construction chemicals sector.
